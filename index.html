why choose kawlify

more affordable

Lots of advertising and marketing companies charge big bucks for advertising. Tens of thousands of dollars? Forget it. They are way too expensive. We understand small local businesses don’t have very big budgets, so we focus on providing smaller budget programs.

focuses on small local businesses and practices

We’re not a large corporation looking to squeeze profits from any kind of business that exists. We like to work with who we have the passion to team up with: the small local businesses, some of which are run by families, that preserve the culture of a community. kawlify focuses on helping these small local businesses like yours: the ones most hurt by Covid.

experience in the professional service industries

Before expanding kawlify into new markets, the founder served as the Marketing Manager at Girard Sharp LLP, a national class action law firm specializing in consumer protection. An example of experience: Due to one of the founder's Facebook ad campaigns using the firm’s budget, the founder was able to attract around 2,400 national potential clients who called or emailed the firm the following weekend regarding a case against a large corporation.
